About Teri

Teri Hart is the embodiment of a dual passion for music and the open road. Born with a guitar in hand and an unyielding wanderlust, Teri's life revolves around the harmony of music and the freedom of trucking. As a singer-songwriter, her soulful melodies and heartfelt lyrics resonate deeply, painting stories that touch the core of human emotions. Off the stage, she swaps the spotlight for the driver's seat of her trusty truck, cruising through highways and embracing the ever-changing landscapes that stretch before her.

Musical Journey

From intimate coffeehouse gigs to sold-out concert halls, Teri's musical journey spans heartfelt ballads, foot-stomping country tunes, and soulful melodies that speak volumes. Her songs reflect her life's adventures, weaving tales of love, longing, and the nomadic spirit that fuels her soul.

Trucking Adventures

Teri's love for the road is as strong as her love for music. When she's not strumming chords, she's navigating the highways, captivated by the endless miles, breathtaking vistas, and the serenity found on the open road.
